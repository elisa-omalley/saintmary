What You'll Find

This website was created to present how we look at cemeteries in a new way. It is just a sample of a bigger project.

By inputting the headstones into a map, it is now easier for one to find exactly the grave they are looking for.

The use of the timeline adds a humanistic aspect to the headstones, by organazing them based on their death date and including any inscriptions.
